CyberFlow- Field®

Open-Field Smart Irrigation. Solar & Wireless.

Open field automatic controller. Keep irrigation controller in your hands anywhere while saving water and energy.

How It Works

  • Each riser pipe has a stand alone controller powered via a photovoltaic panel to control back to back solenoid valves.
  • Each solenoid valve could be programmed separately.
  • Pump motor controller is included.
  • Synchronizing pump control with valves protect your lines from water hammer.

Major Features

  • Wireless controller, no wiring hassle around the field.
  • Water / weather proof enclosers.
  • Ready to connect sensors / weather station.
  • Easy online /offline access.
  • Water saving irrigation system.
  • Easy and Flexible programing app.
  • All year programming, five programs for each zone.
  • Easy to customize upon request.
